Three one-line calls to an extracted function. Not duplication â€” middleware would add complexity for no benefit.

### ~~16. Wrong env var in docs~~ RESOLVED

Fixed in round 2 audit. The env var is `KELS_MAX_VERIFICATION_PAGES` (default 512). The previous resolution incorrectly claimed `KELS_MAX_PAGES_PER_KEL` was correct.
